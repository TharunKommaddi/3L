Priya & Rahul

A Traditional Love Story

Date

December 15, 2025

Venue

Grand Palace, Kadapa

Guests

500+ Guests

Theme

Traditional Red & Gold

Their Story

Priya and Rahul's wedding was a magnificent celebration of tradition and love. Set in the beautiful Grand Palace of Kadapa, the ceremony brought together family and friends from across the country. The bride looked stunning in her red and gold lehenga, while the groom was equally dashing in his traditional sherwani. From the vibrant mehndi ceremony to the emotional bidaai, every moment was filled with joy, laughter, and happy tears. The grand reception featured live music, traditional dance performances, and a spectacular fireworks display that lit up the night sky.

A Destination Wedding Dream

Date

November 28, 2025

Venue

Heritage Resort, Tirupati

Guests

300 Guests

Theme

Royal Blue & Ivory

Their Story

Anjali and Karthik chose the stunning hills of Tirupati for their destination wedding, creating an unforgettable experience for all their guests. The three-day celebration began with a colorful sangeet night featuring Bollywood performances and live music. The wedding ceremony took place at sunrise, with the golden morning light creating a magical ambiance. The reception was an elegant affair with sophisticated décor, gourmet cuisine, and heartfelt speeches that moved everyone to tears. Every detail was perfectly planned, from the floral arrangements to the custom wedding favors, making it truly a celebration to remember.

Deepika & Arjun

A Modern Fusion Celebration

Date

October 20, 2025

Venue

Sky Garden, Hyderabad

Guests

400 Guests

Theme

Blush Pink & Gold

Their Story

Deepika and Arjun's wedding was a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. Held at the luxurious Sky Garden in Hyderabad, the celebration featured contemporary décor with traditional elements. The couple exchanged vows during a beautiful sunset ceremony on the rooftop, with the city skyline as their backdrop. The fusion theme extended to the cuisine, music, and even the couple's attire - Deepika wore a stunning contemporary lehenga for the ceremony and changed into a elegant gown for the reception. The evening concluded with a DJ party that had all guests dancing under a canopy of fairy lights and stars.
